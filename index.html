Mastering Personal Progression

Leveling in the current Alpha build relies on a hybrid system. While quests provide structure, the "Node" system drives the availability of content. Unlike traditional theme-park MMOs, you cannot simply follow a single quest chain. You must diversify your activities to maximize XP per hour. Building a Legacy: Guild Leveling

Guild leveling in Ashes of Creation is a collective effort. It unlocks passive buffs, guild halls, and castle sieging capabilities. For a guild of 30-50 people, coordination is key. Passive play won't cut it; you need focused "Guild Quest" days. The chart below visualizes the theoretical weekly XP output based on active member participation.

Core Mechanics

  • Quest Turn-ins: Specific "Guild Commission" quests grant Guild XP directly.
  • Leveling Up: When a member levels up a character or profession, the guild gains a flat XP bonus.
  • PvP Objectives: Capturing Castles or successful Caravan runs inject massive Guild XP.

The 30-50 Member Sweet Spot

With 30-50 members, you are in the ideal range for the Alpha. You have enough people to run 4-6 full parties simultaneously for Guild Commissions without the logistical nightmare of a 300-person zerg.

The "Golden Path": Speed Meta

Time is precious. Whether you are rushing for "Server First" or just want to reach end-game PvP, efficiency is paramount. The following strategies assume you have a dedicated group (Static) and are willing to skip dialogue.

The "AoE Grind" Loop (Characters)

1

Levels 1-10: The Sprint

Ignore gathering. Grab the Main Story Quest (MSQ) immediately. Complete side quests ONLY if they overlap exactly with MSQ kill areas. between level 6 and 10 finding areas with densely packed enemies and grouping up to level 10 is also viable. Do not travel for side quests.

2

Levels 10-25: Elite Mob Farming

Stop questing solo. Form a group of 8 (Full Party). Find "Elite" or "Dungeon" marked POIs on the map. Round up 5-10 mobs, AoE them down. TIP This is 40% faster than walking between quest givers.

3

Node Management

Stay in the highest level Node available. Higher Node tiers spawn higher density mobs. Move your group to a new Node if the current one stagnates.

The "Commission Blitz" (Guilds)

1

The 30-Man Split

Divide your 30-50 members into fixed 5-man "Commission Squads". Do not let members solo. Squads clear guild tasks 5x faster than individuals.

2

Resource Funneling

Select 3 Crafters in the guild. ALL other 47 members funnel raw materials to them. Power-leveling these crafters unlocks "Master Requests" which grant huge Guild XP bounties.

3

Caravan Cycling

Run Commodities Caravans during off-peak hours (early morning). Even short distance runs grant flat Guild XP. Rotate members to guard/drive.
